Adjusting controls

Desktop
  • Drag a knob or counter up / down to change its value
  • Scroll wheel over a control to step one increment
  • Shift + drag for fine (10× slower) adjustment
Mobile / Touch
  • Touch and drag up / down on any knob or counter
  • Drag slowly for fine control

Tuner model

  • Use the dropdown in the header to switch models
  • Each model has its own controls, factory suggestions, and saved presets

Factory Suggestions

  • Click any row to instantly load its values onto the controls
  • Use as a starting point, then fine-tune to your antenna

My Presets

  • Dial in your settings, enter a name and click + Save
  • Click a saved row to recall it
  • The Modified bar appears when you've drifted from the loaded preset — use Update, Save as new, or Revert

Persistence

  • Your current model and all knob values are saved automatically
  • Reopening the page restores exactly where you left off
